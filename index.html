Secure Your Property

Get Started Checklist

Follow these essential steps to fully secure your property in Ghana. Click each step to track your progress and ensure nothing is missed.

0% Complete - Start your journey!
1

Initial Consultation & Budget Planning

Meet with our experts to discuss your property goals, budget, and preferred locations. We provide free consultation to understand your needs.

2

Site Visit & Property Inspection

Visit the property with our agents. Inspect the land boundaries, neighborhood, road access, and available amenities. Wednesdays & Fridays 10am.

3

Land Search & Title Verification

We conduct thorough land title searches at the Lands Commission to confirm ownership, check for disputes, and ensure the land is litigation-free.

4

Professional Land Surveying

Our licensed surveyors demarcate boundaries, prepare site plans, and ensure accurate measurements that match the title documents.

5

Property Valuation & Negotiation

Get a professional valuation report to ensure fair pricing. We negotiate on your behalf to secure the best deal possible.

6

Documentation & Legal Transfer

We handle all legal paperwork — indentures, leases, assignments, and registration at the Lands Commission to transfer ownership legally.

7

Final Registration & Handover

Receive your title certificate and complete handover. We also assist with development planning and contractor recommendations.
